Original wildlife, portraiture, and landscape photography

All photographs in this collection are the original work of Claire Sanderson.

Hardware: Canon EOS 80D 24.2 MP Digital SLR

Claire Sanderson Photography aims to capture people, environment, and wildlife in a candid setting. She took on the hobby of amateur photography in 2005 as a way of documenting her travels and milestones. Since then, the artist has traveled throughout five continents, capturing the local environment through the lense of a Canon.

The photos on this site were predominantly taken in Southwest Virginia and Kasane, Botswana; the regions in which the artist lives and works depending on the time of year.
